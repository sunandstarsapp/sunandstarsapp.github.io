Helps children sleep and know when it's time to get up.

Upcycle your old android or iOS phone or tablet into a sleep trainer with the Sun & Stars app.

Sleep and Wake

When it's time to wake up, the app will automatically switch from the dark screen with the moon to a bright yellow screen with a sun, and will also play the music selected in the settings screen.

It's not time to get up until the sun comes out!

At night time, the moon communicates sleep, and soothing music can be played by tapping the screen.

Settings

Set time to wake up, and time to go to sleep.

Choose how long to play music for on wake time, sleep time, or screen tap.

Choose between no sound, original music by L. Chenier, the white noise of a fan, or a playlist from iTunes (iOS only).

Features:

  • Glowing screen shows images of moon/stars and sun to communicate ‘sleep’ and ‘wake-up’ time
  • Upcycles your old phone to use with the APP – use an old phone/tablet/apple products with an IOS of 9.0 or newer, or android 4.1 or newer
  • Can be used in either portrait or landscape mode
  • Suitable from age 18 months + 
  • Can be used by children and adults as the change in colors assist with wake and sleep cues for your body
  • Helps teach time with the digital clock
  • Easily programmable for wake and sleep modes (simple setting options allow for quick and easy resets for weekends or special days)
  • One touch operation to the sun or moon to change from night to day and vice-versa (e.g. for early bedtimes and early wake times)
  • Created/designed/programmed locally in Edmonton, Alberta Canada.   Contemporary music composed and recorded by L. Chenier in Edmonton.
