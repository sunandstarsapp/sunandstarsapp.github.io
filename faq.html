Frequently Asked Questions

Why didn't the music stop after the time I set in the settings?
Actual playtime of songs will be slightly longer then what is set in the settings because the app will finish the currently playing song before stopping the music.

How to I get out of full screen mode to get to the settings?
Tap and hold anywhere on the screeen to bring the menu back up. Press the icon in the top right on the main clock screen to return to full screen mode.
